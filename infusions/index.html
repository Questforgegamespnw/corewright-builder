Builder Infusions Subclass

Corewright Infusion Compendium

Tank

Effect: The golem’s hit point maximum increases by an amount equal to your artificer level, and it gains a +1 bonus to Armor Class.

Details: This bonus is applied when the golem is created and scales automatically as you gain levels. This represents additional structural reinforcement, plating, or dense material layering.

DPS

Effect: When commanded to take the Attack action, the golem can make two attacks instead of one. Each attack deals an additional 1d6 damage.

Details: This additional damage applies to each successful hit. The damage type matches the attack unless otherwise specified.

Utility

Effect: The golem’s Dexterity becomes 14 unless already higher. It gains proficiency with simple weapons and can wield weapons and manipulate objects.

Details: The golem replaces its natural attacks with weapon attacks. It can take the Use an Object action without requiring your command.

Utility

Effect: The golem’s movement speed increases by 10 feet.

Details: It can take the Dash or Disengage action as a bonus action without requiring your command.

Tank

Effect: When the golem takes damage, it can use its reaction to reduce that damage by 1d10 + your proficiency bonus.

Details: This can be used after damage is rolled but before it is applied.

Utility

Effect: When you cast a spell with a range of touch, your golem can deliver the spell.

Details: The golem must be within 30 feet of you. The spell originates from the golem’s position.

Tank Control

Effect: The golem has advantage on saving throws against being knocked prone, pushed, or pulled.

Details: Forced movement affecting the golem is reduced to a maximum of 10 feet.

DPS

Effect: Once per turn, when the golem hits a creature, it deals an additional 10 force damage.

Details: After using this feature, the golem takes force damage equal to your proficiency bonus.

Control

Effect: When a creature within 5 feet of the golem attacks a target other than it, the golem can use its reaction to make a melee attack.

Details: This occurs after the triggering attack is declared.

Advanced Infusions (9th Level)

Tank

Effect: At the start of each of its turns, the golem regains hit points equal to your Intelligence modifier + proficiency bonus.

Details: This regeneration is suppressed until the end of its next turn if it takes acid or fire damage.

Tank

Effect: At the start of each of its turns, the golem regains hit points equal to your Intelligence modifier + proficiency bonus.

Details: This regeneration is suppressed until the end of its next turn if it takes acid or fire damage.

DPS

Effect: The golem deals double damage to objects and structures.

Details: Its attacks deal an additional 1d8 damage, but attack rolls against it have advantage until the start of its next turn.

Control

Effect: When a creature misses the golem, it can use its reaction to make a melee attack.

Details: This can trigger once per round.

Masterwork Infusions (15th Level)

Utility

Effect: The golem gains proficiency in all saving throws.

Details: It adds your Intelligence modifier to its ability checks and saving throws and can take the Help action as a bonus action without your command.

Utility

Effect: As a bonus action, the golem becomes incorporeal until the start of its next turn.

Details: It can move through creatures and objects as difficult terrain.

DPS

Effect: When commanded to attack, the golem makes one additional attack.

Details: At the end of its turn, it takes force damage equal to your proficiency bonus.

Tank

Effect: When the golem takes damage, it can use its reaction to gain resistance to that damage type.

Details: This resistance lasts until the end of its next turn.

Utility

Effect: As an action, the golem creates a duplicate in an adjacent space.

Details: The duplicate has hit points equal to your artificer level and lasts for 1 minute.

DPS

Effect: When the golem deals damage matching its Engine Core type, it deals additional damage equal to your Intelligence modifier.

Details: If it damages multiple creatures, it gains temporary hit points equal to your proficiency bonus.

Tank DPS

Effect: The golem’s size increases by one category (max Huge).

Details: It gains increased hit points, Strength, Constitution, and reach, but suffers reduced speed and disadvantage on Dexterity saving throws.
