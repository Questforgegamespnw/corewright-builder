Builder Infusions Subclass

Corewright Infusion Compendium

Tank

Effect: The golem’s hit point maximum increases by an amount equal to your artificer level, and it gains a +1 bonus to Armor Class.

Details: Applied at creation and scales automatically with level.

DPS

Effect: When commanded to take the Attack action, the golem can make two attacks instead of one. Each attack deals an additional 1d6 damage.

Details: Damage type matches the attack unless specified otherwise.

Utility

Effect: The golem’s Dexterity becomes 14 unless higher. Gains proficiency with simple weapons and can manipulate objects.

Details: Can take the Use an Object action without requiring your command.

Utility

Effect: Golem movement speed increases by 10 feet.

Details: Can take Dash or Disengage as a bonus action.

Tank

Effect: When the golem takes damage, it can use its reaction to reduce that damage by 1d10 + your proficiency bonus.

Details: Use after damage is rolled but before applied.

Utility

Effect: Your golem can deliver touch-range spells you cast.

Details: Golem must be within 30 feet; spell originates from golem.

Advanced Infusions (9th Level)

Tank

Effect: At the start of each turn, golem regains hit points equal to your Int modifier + proficiency bonus.

Details: Regeneration suppressed until end of next turn if taking acid or fire damage.

DPS

Effect: Golem deals double damage to objects and structures.

Details: Attacks deal +1d8 damage; attack rolls against it have advantage until next turn.

Control

Effect: When a creature misses the golem, it can make a melee attack as a reaction.

Details: Can trigger once per round.

Masterwork Infusions (15th Level)

Utility

Effect: Golem gains proficiency in all saving throws.

Details: Adds your Int modifier to ability checks & saving throws; can take Help action as bonus action.

Utility

Effect: Golem becomes incorporeal as a bonus action until next turn.

Details: Can move through creatures and objects (difficult terrain).
