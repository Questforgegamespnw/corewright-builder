Tank

Details: Damage type matches the attack unless stated otherwise.

Utility

Details: Can take Use an Object action independently.

Utility

Details: Can Dash or Disengage as a bonus action.

Tank

Effect: Reaction to reduce damage by 1d10 + proficiency bonus.

Details: Can be used after damage is rolled but before applied.

Utility

Effect: Deliver touch-range spells via the golem.

Details: Golem must be within 30 ft; spell originates from it.

Tank Control

Effect: Advantage on saving throws vs. knock prone/push/pull.

Details: Forced movement capped at 10 ft.

DPS

Effect: On hit, +10 force damage once per turn.

Details: After use, golem takes force damage = proficiency bonus.

Control

Effect: Reaction attack if creature attacks others within 5 ft of golem.
