Artificer Specialist: Corewright

Corewrights are artificers who pierce the boundary between construct and life, crafting bodies of stone, metal, and sinew, and animating them with arcane cores of immense power. These constructs are evolving engines of will, shaped by their creator’s design.

Golem Core Creation (3rd Level)

You construct a magical core that animates a golem.

  • Create or rebuild during a long rest
  • Only one active golem
  • Acts on your initiative
  • Bonus action to command
  • Takes Dodge if not commanded

Golem Infusions (5th Level)

You learn specialized modifications for your golem.

  • Apply during creation
  • Persist until changed
  • Multiple infusions can be active

Golem Templates (9th Level)

Choose a material form such as stone, metal, wood, or more exotic types.

  • Defines base defenses
  • Grants passive traits

Living Engine Core (15th Level)

Your golem gains an elemental engine.

  • Flame: Fire damage and aura
  • Stone: Damage reduction and stability
  • Frost: Slows enemies
  • Aether: Mobility and flight

Master Golemmancy (17th Level)

  • Dual Core: Two golems
  • Fusion: Merge with golem
  • Autonomous: Golem acts independently
