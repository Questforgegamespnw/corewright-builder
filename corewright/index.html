Artificer Specialist: Corewright

Corewrights are artificers who pierce the boundary between construct and life, crafting bodies of stone, metal, and sinew, and animating them with arcane cores of immense power. These constructs are evolving engines of will, shaped by their creator’s design.

Corewright Spells

You always have these spells prepared. They don’t count against your prepared spells.

  • 1st Level: Alarm, Tenser’s Floating Disk, Unseen Servant
  • 2nd Level: Enlarge/Reduce, Heat Metal, Enhance Ability
  • 3rd Level: Protection from Energy, Tiny Servant, Conjure Constructs*
  • 4th Level: Stone Shape, Elemental Bane, Summon Construct
  • 5th Level: Transmute Rock, Animate Objects

*Conjure Constructs is a homebrew spell.

Golem Core Creation (3rd Level)

You construct a magical core that animates a golem.

  • Create or rebuild during a long rest
  • Only one active golem
  • Acts on your initiative
  • Bonus action to command
  • Takes Dodge if not commanded

Golem Infusions (5th Level)

You learn specialized modifications for your golem.

  • Apply during creation
  • Persist until changed
  • Multiple infusions can be active

Golem Templates (9th Level)

Choose a material form such as stone, metal, wood, or more exotic types.

  • Defines base defenses
  • Grants passive traits

Living Engine Core (15th Level)

Your golem gains an elemental engine.

  • Flame: Fire damage and aura
  • Stone: Damage reduction and stability
  • Frost: Slows enemies
  • Aether: Mobility and flight

17th-Level Feature: Penultimate Golemmancy

At 17th level, you achieve the pinnacle of your craft, unlocking the ultimate potential of your Golem Core. Choose one of the following options, representing the final evolution of your construct mastery:

  • Core Fusion
  • Awakened Core
  • Dual Core Convergence

Once you make this choice, it cannot be changed.

🔥 Core Fusion

You can merge with your golem, becoming a single, empowered construct.

Bonus Action: Merge with your golem. This lasts 1 minute, until you end it, or until you are reduced to 0 hit points.

While fused:

  • Gain temporary hit points equal to your golem’s current hit points
  • Retain your statistics but gain Golem Infusions, Material Template, and Living Engine Core
  • Melee attacks deal +1d8 damage of your Engine Core type
  • Speed increases by 10 ft and gain any special movement your golem possesses
  • Attack action grants one additional weapon attack

When the transformation ends, your golem reappears within 5 feet of you with half its maximum hit points.

Recharge: Long rest

🧠 Awakened Core

Your Golem Core develops true autonomy, allowing your construct to act independently.

  • Golem rolls its own initiative and acts independently
  • No longer requires your bonus action but follows commands
  • Gains saving throw bonus = your Intelligence modifier
  • Can take reactions normally
  • Once per turn, add your Intelligence modifier to one attack or damage roll
  • If you are incapacitated, golem continues defending allies

⚙️ Dual Core Convergence

You can divide your Golem Core, creating multiple constructs.

  • When creating a golem, you may instead create two golems with half normal HP
  • Both golems act on the same initiative, taking turns after yours
  • Command both golems with the same bonus action
  • Each golem makes one attack when taking the Attack action
  • If one golem is destroyed, the other regains HP = your artificer level
  • Whenever you create a golem, choose to make one or two
