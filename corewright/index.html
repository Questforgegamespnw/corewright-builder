Artificer Specialist: Corewright

Corewrights are artificers who pierce the boundary between construct and life, crafting bodies of stone, metal, and sinew, and animating them with arcane cores of immense power. These constructs are evolving engines of will, shaped by their creator’s design.

Corewright Spells

You always have the following spells prepared, and they don’t count against the number of spells you can prepare.

  • 1st Level: Alarm, Tenser’s Floating Disk, Unseen Servant
  • 2nd Level: Enlarge/Reduce, Heat Metal, Locate Object
  • 3rd Level: Protection from Energy, Tiny Servant, Conjure Constructs*
  • 4th Level: Stone Shape, Elemental Bane, Summon Construct
  • 5th Level: Transmute Rock, Animate Objects

*Conjure Constructs is a homebrew spell.

Golem Core Creation (3rd Level)

  • Create or rebuild during a long rest
  • Only one active golem
  • Acts on your initiative
  • Bonus action to command
  • Takes Dodge if not commanded

Golem Infusions (5th Level)

  • Apply during creation
  • Persist until changed
  • Multiple infusions can be active

Golem Templates (9th Level)

  • Defines base defenses
  • Grants passive traits

Living Engine Core (15th Level)

  • Flame: Fire damage and aura
  • Stone: Damage reduction and stability
  • Frost: Slows enemies
  • Aether: Mobility and flight

17th-Level Feature: Penultimate Golemmancy

Choose one final evolution for your construct:

  • Core Fusion
  • Awakened Core
  • Dual Core Convergence

Once chosen, it cannot be changed.

🔥 Core Fusion

  • Merge with your golem, becoming a single empowered construct.
  • Bonus Action: Merge (lasts 1 minute or until 0 HP)
  • While fused: gain temporary HP equal to golem, retain stats, gain Golem Infusions, Material Template, and Engine Core, extra 1d8 damage on melee, +10 ft speed, extra weapon attack.
  • When ended, golem reappears with half max HP.
  • Recharge: Long rest

🧠 Awakened Core

  • Golem acts independently, rolls initiative, and follows commands without bonus action.
  • Bonus to saving throws equal to your INT modifier.
  • Once per turn, add INT modifier to one attack or damage roll.
  • If incapacitated, golem continues defending allies.

⚙️ Dual Core Convergence

  • Create two golems with half HP each.
  • Both act on same initiative; command both with one bonus action.
  • Each can make one attack per Attack action.
  • If one is destroyed, other regains HP equal to your artificer level.
